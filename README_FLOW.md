# Carbon Footprint Calculator - User Flow Guide

This document describes the step-by-step flow of the Carbon Footprint Calculator application, designed to help users track and understand their environmental impact.

## 1. Getting Started
*   **Authentication**: Upon opening the application, new users must **Register** with their details. Returning users can **Login** to access their account.
*   **Secure Access**: All user data is private and protected, requiring a login to view personal carbon footprint history.

## 2. Dashboard (Home)
Once logged in, you land on the **Dashboard**, which serves as your personal environmental overview.
*   **Quick Summary**: Instantly see your latest carbon footprint score and whether it falls into a "Low Impact" or "Needs Improvement" category.
*   **Visual Insights**:
    *   **Emission Sources**: A pie chart visualizes which areas of your life (e.g., Home Energy, Transport, Diet) contribute most to your emissions.
    *   **History Trend**: A line graph tracks your carbon footprint over time, showing your progress in reducing emissions.
*   **Goals**: Track your progress towards a reduction goal (e.g., aiming for a 15% reduction).

## 3. Calculator
The core feature of the app is the **Carbon Footprint Calculator**. It guides you through a simple 4-step wizard to estimate your annual CO2 emissions.

### Step 1: Home Energy
Focuses on your household energy consumption.
*   **Electricity Usage**: Enter your average monthly electricity consumption (in kWh).
*   **Cooking Fuel**: Select the type of fuel used for cooking (LPG, Electric, Piped Gas).
*   **Household Size**: Number of people in your home (to divide shared usage).
*   **AC Usage**: Frequency of Air Conditioner usage.

### Step 2: Transport
Estimates emissions from your daily commute and travel.
*   **Primary Transport**: Choose your main mode of travel (Car, Bike, Bus, Train, Walk/Cycle).
*   **Distance**: Average distance travelled per month.
*   **Vehicle Ownership**: Whether you own the vehicle.
*   **Flights**: Number of flights taken per year.

### Step 3: Diet & Lifestyle
Looks at personal habits that affect your footprint.
*   **Diet Type**: Select your diet (Mixed, Vegetarian, Vegan, Heavy Meat Eater).
*   **Consumption Habits**: Frequency of non-veg meals (if applicable).
*   **Shopping Habits**: How frequently you buy new clothes or gadgets.
*   **Waste Management**: Whether you segregate your waste for recycling.

### Step 4: Results & Recommendations
After completing the survey, you receive an instant analysis.
*   **Total Score**: Your estimated annual carbon footprint in kg CO2.
*   **Comparison**: See if your footprint has increased or decreased compared to your last check.
*   **Category Breakdown**: Detailed numbers for Energy, Transport, Diet, and Lifestyle.
*   **Personalized Recommendations**: Smart tips to reduce your impact based on your highest emission categories (e.g., "Switch to LED bulbs" if energy usage is high).

## 4. History
The **History** section creates a timeline of your environmental journey.
*   **Trend Analysis**: View a graph of your past calculations to visualize your long-term progress.
*   **Past Records List**: A detailed list of every calculation you've made, including the date and the specific breakdown for that day.
*   **Motivation**: Tracks how many times you've calculated your footprint, encouraging consistent monitoring.
